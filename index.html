About Our College

Loyola Degree College YSRR

Loyola Degree College YSRR is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality education and fostering intellectual growth. With experienced faculty and modern infrastructure, we create an environment where students can excel academically and develop as responsible citizens.

Our Mission: To impart quality education that enables students to face the challenges of the modern world with confidence and integrity.

Our Vision: To be a center of excellence in higher education, producing graduates who are competent, ethical, and committed to nation-building.

Our Programs

📚 Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) Programs

MPC (Mathematics, Physics & Chemistry)

A comprehensive science program focusing on core mathematics, physics, and chemistry fundamentals.

MPCS (Mathematics, Physics & Computer Science)

Combines mathematics and physics with cutting-edge computer science applications.

MPST (Mathematics, Physics & Statistics)

Advanced program integrating mathematics, physics, and statistical analysis.

MSCS (Mathematics, Statistics & Computer Science)

Specialization in data science combining mathematics, statistics, and programming.

BZC (Botany, Zoology & Chemistry)

Comprehensive life sciences program with emphasis on biological and chemical concepts.

CZC (Chemistry, Zoology & Computer Applications)

Blend of life sciences with modern computer applications for research and analysis.

GPC (Geology, Physics & Computer Applications)

Earth sciences program with physics and computational tools for geological studies.

🎨 Bachelor of Arts (B.A) Programs

HPCA (History, Political Science & Computer Applications)

Social sciences program combining history and political science with digital literacy and applications.

💼 Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) Programs

B.Com with Computer Applications

Commerce education integrated with modern IT skills for digital business transformation.

🏢 Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

BBA (General)

Comprehensive business administration course preparing students for corporate leadership and entrepreneurship.
